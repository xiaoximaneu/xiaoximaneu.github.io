Welcome to VoteChat

By Northeastern University Media Innovation Studio

Published 03/08, 2022

The Emancipator are so excited to introduce you to VoteChat, an interactive tool to inform young and first-time voters on current and pending voting laws, resources on how to vote, and ways to take action in your community.

Firstly, let's take a look at the demo:

Now, you may be wondering what kind of information you'd get from VoteChat.

You will find information including but not limited to :

  • current and pending voting laws
  • resources on how to vote
  • ways to take action in your community

Quick FAQs:

"How can I find votechat?"

- You may find it on platforms like Facebook and SMS.

"Do I need to pay for it?"

- "No! It is completely free for you."

"How reliable is the VoteChat?"

- VoteChat are not just using data from States' official website, but also Brennan Center and Ballotpedia. The data will always be updated to the current state so it will be your reliable friend.
