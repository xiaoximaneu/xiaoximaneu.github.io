Quick FAQs:

1. How can I find votechat?

You may find it on platforms like Facebook and SMS.

2. Do I have to pay for it?

No, it is completely free for you.

3. How reliable is the VoteChat?

VoteChat are not just using data from states official website, but also Brennan Center and Ballotpedia. The data will always be updated to the current state so it will be your reliable friend.
