Her World

A Glimpse into the World of Top Women Chess Players Worldwide

Created by Xiaoxi Ma


The Queen's Gambit is an American TV miniseries from 2020 that captured everyone's attention at the time. However, after the craze wore off, I wonder what the real world of the world's top female chess players looked like. We will try to use this dataset of top women Chess players and the up-to-date ranking data of The International Chess Federation(FIDE) to see the world of these amamzing female players. Of course we will have some questions about them, so let's see if this data can tell us more.
