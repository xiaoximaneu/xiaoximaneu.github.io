Her World

A Glimpse into the World of Top Women Chess Players Worldwide

Created by Xiaoxi Ma


The Queen's Gambit is an American TV miniseries from 2020 that captured everyone's attention at the time. However, after the craze wore off, I wonder what the real world of the world's top female chess players looked like. We will try to use this dataset of top women Chess players and the till-this-April ranking data of The International Chess Federation(FIDE) to see the world of these amazing female players. Let's see what these data will tell us.
